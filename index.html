What do I do?

MY MISSION IS TO INSPIRE, MENTOR AND MOTIVATE.

Arosto Ben is a notable youth counsellor who have dedicated his life to helping the youth discover themselves and be the best in life. Having trained as a teacher, certified by TSC – Kenya, and also a career counsellor, he has had an opportunity to mingle with many youths making him aware of the challenges that they are experiencing in this 21st century. He has helped hundreds of the youths excel in their academic life, besides helping them choose careers that they best fit in. He has also had an opportunity to work with different schools and Churches in Kenya. Comprehensively, he is passionate on matters surrounding the youths, Marriage and Career.
